ABOUT US

Best Optometrist & Eye Care That You Can Trust

We understand that every individual's vision needs are unique. Our optometrists create personalized treatment plans tailored to your specific requirements. Whether you need prescription glasses, contact lenses, or specialized treatments, we provide customized solutions to help you achieve your best vision.

We are an in-network provider for National Health Insurance Scheme (NHIS) and some Health Maintenance Organizations (HMO). Smiley Optical Services work with many health insurance providers to ensure good eye health and vision care for all of our patients. We have a retainership with NMDPRA Nigerian Midstream and Downstream Petroleum Agency, Amcon and also signed up with a number of Health Management Organization (HMO) including: Avon, Axa Mansard, Hygeia, Total Health Trust, Venus Medicare, Mediplan, Precious Health, Reliance, Songhia, United Health Care, NNPC, Fountain, Leadway, Wellness, Aman, Prepaid, MB &O, Routhage, Hallmark, and more. If you have other types of coverage that are not listed here, contact us.
